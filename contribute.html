Donate

It takes financial contributions to sustain the church building and the work we do. Please help by donating to the following projects and keep the mission of the parish going.

Most especially during this pandemic. The order to shelter in place does not apply to monies needed for payroll, The Plants, regular bills, disinfectants and everything else it takes to keep St. Benedict the African running and ready for your return.

Please consider giving towards the following projects at St. Benedict the African. You will be redirected to the GiveCentral website, an online donation tool, that allows the parish to fundraise more efficiently and securely.

Sunday Giving

Please consider signing up for online contribution for your weekly tithing. It's secure, convenient, and easy.

Donate Now

Father Augustus Tolton

Help us support the cause for canonization for the first recognized American diocesan priest of African descent. Let us support the Sainthood of a past Chicago and Illinois priest.

Donate Now

The Arts

Praise Break & Jam is a platform for young artists who love to praise with their music, break it down to the people, and jam out with their audiences.

Donate Now

You can support more of our other fundraising opportunities at our GiveCentral page.

Paypal allows you to send a donation to SBAparish@aol.com through the Paypal website or app.

You can text the word "tithe" to (312)548-6599 and follow the instructions to donate.

Volunteer

Saint Benedict the African also welcomes your gift of time and talents. Please contact us if you can support the church in other ways.
