Donate

It takes financial contributions to sustain the church building and work we do. Please help by donating to the projects and keep the mission of the parish going.

You will be redirected to the GiveCentral website, an online donation tool, that allows the parish to fundraise more efficiently and securely.

Sunday Giving

Please consider signing up for online contribution for your weekly tithing. It's secure, convenient, and easy.

Father Augustus Tolton

Help us support the cause for canonization for the first recognized American diocesan priest of African descent. Let us support the Sainthood of a past Chicago and Illinois priest.

Other Donations

Any one-time contribution amount no matter how small helps in keeping the church alive.

View more of our other fundraising opportunities you can support at our GiveCentral page.

Volunteer

Saint Benedict the African also welcomes your gift of time and talents. Please contact us if you can support the church in other ways.
